Open access endpoints

These endpoints are unrestricted and service general data held by us. They don't allow you to access secure taxpayer data.

Example

curl -H 'Content-Type: application/vnd.hmrc.v1+json' \
https://api.service.hmrc.gov.uk/hello/world

For a working example see the open access endpoint tutorial.
